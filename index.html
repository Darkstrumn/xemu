X-Emulators ~ Xemu

Emulators running on Linux/Unix/Windows/OSX of various (mainly 8 bit) machines, including the Commodore LCD, Commodore 65 and Mega 65 as well.

Note: there is nothing too much common in these machines. The only reason that I emulate these within a single project, that I can easily re-use some of the components needed, that's all!
